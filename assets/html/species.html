Species

Colors of the World project aims to create suitable conditions for the growth of endemic plant species within the campus. By conducting thorough research on their specific requirements, we aim to promote the growth and spread of these endemic plants, thus contributing to plant biodiversity and minimizing the risk of their extinction. Followings are the some endemic plant species.
